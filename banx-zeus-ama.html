Banx & Zeus Network AMA Summary

Browse the important details from the Banx & Zeus AMA or download the full transcript below.


Banx Overview


  • Background: Banx started as an NFT project and evolved into focusing on NFT lending to bring utility to the Solana NFT space.
  • Challenges: The team highlighted the challenges of building on a new chain like Solana and the importance of NFT and Solana's success for their project.
  • Community and Education: Emphasized the role of community building and education in their growth strategy. Recently launched the Banx.edu protocol to enhance user understanding of their platform.
  • Future Plans: Looking to expand offerings beyond NFTs, exploring liquid staking and USDC loans.

Zeus Network Overview


  • Mission: Zeus Network aims to link Solana and Bitcoin, leveraging the strengths of both to create a high-performance decentralized trading environment.
  • Technical Approach: Utilizing Solana's virtual machine (SVM) for seamless and permissionless communication between the two chains.
  • Challenges: Addressed the complexity of creating a communication layer between different blockchains and the need for a secure, decentralized solution.
  • Future Vision: Envisions a future where multiple blockchains can communicate and interact seamlessly through Zeus Network, with a focus on security and decentralization.
