Julian joins the call to report that 343 respondents participated in the post-voting survey, with results similar to actual voting. Those who voted for the proposal cited belief in rewarding good work, trust in the vision, and a desire for community growth.

LFG Candidate: DeBridge

Alex joins the call to talk to us about deBridge. deBridge started building on Solana in 2021 with an SF grant, launching on Solana in June 2023. They have since onboarded 80,000 users, with 1,000 new users joining daily.

Processing 7-figure transfers with minimal price impact, deBridge launched IaaS for interoperability between SVM and EVM. They collaborate with Solana partners like Jupiter, Solflare, Zeta Markets, and Birdeye.

LFG Candidate: Exchange.art

Milst joins the call to introduce Exchange.art! Launched in 2021, creating tools for independent artists and collectors. In 2023, they introduced the first Long-Form Gen Art platform on Solana, becoming the largest art marketplace on Solana.

By the numbers, Exchange.Art has achieved $27M in art sales, sold 350K artworks, and registered 25K artists and 50K collectors. They have hosted over 20 in-real-life exhibitions worldwide.

LFG Candidate: Divvy.bet

Loso joins to discuss DivvyBet, a decentralized betting protocol with bets that are 100% anonymous and secured by smart contracts. Since their mainnet launch in February 2023, they have processed over $4 million in betting volume and facilitated over 40k unique wagers.

Future plans include DCA, limit orders, and token discovery features.
